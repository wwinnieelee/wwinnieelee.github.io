Banana Blueberry Oatmeal

It is gluten-free ! Oatmeal provides fiber and antioxidants which benefits those who wants to slim down or toned up their body !
** It only takes 3 mins to prepare ! **
Researchers also found that boosting fiber by 8g for every 1,000 calories resulted in about 4.5 pounds of weight lost!
How to prepare ?

    Ingredients :
  • > 35g oats
  • > 1 banana
  • > 10g blueberries

Cocoa-Chia Pudding with Raspberries

Chocolate is healthy for us! Especially when having chocolate for breakfast with this chia pudding recipe. The deep chocolaty flavor pairs seamlessly with juicy raspberries for a fun-switch-up from oatmeal for your morning routine.
How to prepare?

    Ingredients :
  • > ½ cup unsweetened almond milk or other nondairy milk
  • > 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • > 2 teaspoons pure maple syrup
  • > ½ teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • > ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • > ½ cup fresh raspberries, divided
  • > 1 tablespoon toasted sliced almonds, divided

Ricotta & Yogurt Parfait

By eating yogurt, it may give you an extra edge if you're looking to get leaner. It contains protein and low in fat. Choose plain yogurt to avoid processed sugar and unnecessary sugar in your body and your daily intake. If you need a little extra sweetness, try fresh fruits(strawberries, raspberries, blueberries...)
This is easy to make by just purchasing any greek yogurt, ricotta, nuts and some fruits (for extra sweetness) near your supermarket or any store nearby.

Peanut Butter-Banana Roll-Ups

Nuts were among the TOP 5 foods that Harvard reserachers said promote weight loss. Slather a tablespoon or two of peanut butter onto whole-wheat toast.
How to prepare?

    Ingredients :
  • > 2 tablespoons smooth natural peanut butter or sunflower seed butter
  • > 1 teaspoon honey
  • > 1 8-inch whole-wheat flour tortilla, at room temperature
  • > 1 medium banana, peeled
